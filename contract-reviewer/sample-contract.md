# Master SaaS Services Agreement

**Between:** Acme Cloud Solutions, Inc. ("Provider")
**And:** [Customer Name] ("Customer")

## 1. Payment Terms

Customer shall pay all invoices within **Net 60** of invoice date. Late payments accrue interest at **1.5% per month** (18% annualized). Provider reserves the right to suspend access after 15 days of non-payment without prior written notice. All fees are non-refundable, including prepaid annual subscriptions, regardless of early termination.

## 2. Term and Auto-Renewal

This Agreement has an initial term of **36 months**. The Agreement will **automatically renew for successive 24-month periods** unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least **180 days** before the end of the current term. During renewal terms, Provider may increase fees by up to **12% per year** without Customer approval.

## 3. Termination

Provider may terminate this Agreement **immediately and without cause** upon 30 days' written notice. Customer may terminate only **for cause** after providing 90 days' written notice and a 60-day cure period. Upon any termination, Customer must pay all remaining fees for the balance of the current term ("early termination fee"). Provider will make Customer data available for export for **15 days** post-termination, after which all data will be permanently deleted.

## 4. Liability Cap

Provider's total aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed **the fees paid by Customer in the 3 months** immediately preceding the claim. This cap applies to all causes of action, including breach of contract, negligence, and strict liability. Provider shall have **no liability** for any indirect, incidental, consequential, special, or punitive damages, including lost profits, lost data, or business interruption, even if advised of the possibility.

## 5. Service Level Agreement (SLA)

Provider targets **99.5% monthly uptime** for the production environment. Scheduled maintenance windows (up to 8 hours per month) are excluded from uptime calculations. If uptime falls below 99.5% in any calendar month, Customer's **sole remedy** is a service credit equal to **5% of that month's fees**, capped at **25% of monthly fees** in any given quarter. Provider is not liable for outages caused by third-party services, including cloud infrastructure providers.

## 6. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep confidential information received from the other party strictly confidential for **2 years** following disclosure. However, Provider may share Customer's confidential information with its **subcontractors, affiliates, and service providers** without prior Customer consent, provided they are bound by "substantially similar" (but not identical) confidentiality obligations. Customer's usage data, analytics, and aggregated statistics are expressly excluded from confidentiality protections and may be used by Provider for any purpose.

## 7. Data Handling and Privacy

Customer grants Provider a **worldwide, royalty-free, perpetual license** to use, process, and analyze Customer data for the purpose of operating, improving, and marketing the Service. Provider may store Customer data in **any geographic region** at its discretion, including regions that may not have equivalent data protection regulations. Provider will use "commercially reasonable" security measures but does not warrant that the service will be free from unauthorized access.

## 8. Intellectual Property

All intellectual property rights in the Service, including any customizations, integrations, or configurations developed for Customer, shall belong **exclusively to Provider**. Any feedback, suggestions, or feature requests submitted by Customer become Provider's property and may be incorporated into the Service without compensation or attribution. Customer retains no rights to request removal of contributed ideas from the platform.

## 9. Non-Compete and Exclusivity

During the term of this Agreement and for **24 months** following termination, Customer agrees not to develop, license, or use any competing software that provides substantially similar functionality to the Service. "Substantially similar" is determined solely at Provider's reasonable discretion. Violation of this clause entitles Provider to liquidated damages equal to **200% of the total contract value**.

## 10. Dispute Resolution

All disputes shall be resolved through **binding arbitration** administered in **Provider's home jurisdiction** (Delaware) under Provider's chosen arbitration rules. Each party bears its own costs, but the losing party shall reimburse the prevailing party's reasonable legal fees. Customer **waives the right** to participate in any class action or collective arbitration. All arbitration proceedings and outcomes shall remain confidential.

## 11. Indemnification

Customer shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Provider against **any and all claims, damages, losses, and expenses** (including attorneys' fees) arising from Customer's use of the Service, Customer's breach of this Agreement, or any third-party claims related to Customer's data. Provider has **no reciprocal indemnification obligation** to Customer.

## 12. Force Majeure

Neither party shall be liable for delays caused by events beyond reasonable control, including natural disasters, war, pandemic, government action, or **Provider's third-party service failures**. During a force majeure event extending beyond **30 days**, Provider may suspend services without obligation to provide credits or refunds. Customer remains obligated to pay all fees during force majeure suspensions.

## 13. Assignment

Provider may freely assign this Agreement to any successor, affiliate, or acquirer **without Customer consent**. Customer may not assign this Agreement without Provider's prior written consent, which may be withheld for any reason. Any attempted assignment by Customer without consent shall be void.

## 14. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the **State of Delaware**, without regard to conflict of law principles. Any legal action must be brought exclusively in the courts of Delaware. Customer consents to personal jurisdiction in Delaware and waives any objection to venue.

## 15. Entire Agreement and Amendments

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties. Provider may modify terms at any time by posting updated terms on its website. Continued use of the Service after posted changes constitutes acceptance. Customer's purchase orders or other documents shall have no legal effect, even if accepted or signed by Provider.
