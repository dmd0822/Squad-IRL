# Grading Rubric

Grade the writer's output on a 100-point scale using this rubric. Be **strict and rigorous**.

## Scoring Breakdown (Total: 100 points)

### Personality & Authenticity (30) (30 points)

Voice feels genuine, not templated; unique personality shines through

### Clarity & Impact (25) (25 points)

Value proposition is immediately clear; accomplishments are specific and impressive

### SEO & Discoverability (20) (20 points)

Keywords integrated naturally; readable by both humans and search engines

### Structure & Scannability (15) (15 points)

Clear hierarchy, short paragraphs, effective use of formatting

### Call-to-Action Effectiveness (10) (10 points)

CTAs are compelling and appropriately placed


## Grading Standards

- **90-100:** Exceptional. Exceeds all requirements with domain expertise evident throughout.
- **80-89:** Strong. Meets all requirements well with minor gaps.
- **70-79:** Adequate. Meets most requirements but has noticeable weaknesses.
- **60-69:** Below expectations. Missing key requirements or significant quality issues.
- **Below 60:** Unacceptable. Major requirements missing or fundamentally flawed.

## Grading Process

1. Evaluate each criterion independently
2. Deduct points for specific failures
3. Do not inflate scores—be honest about weaknesses
4. Provide specific feedback on what was missing or could improve
5. Calculate total score and provide overall assessment

## Output Format

Return a structured grade with:
- Score for each criterion with justification
- Total score
- Key strengths (2-3 points)
- Key weaknesses (2-3 points)
- Specific improvement recommendations
