# Stream Highlight Clipper Grading Rubric

## Moment Selection (30 points)

- **Viral Potential (15 pts)** - Are selected moments genuinely engaging and shareable?
- **Variety (10 pts)** - Is there a good mix of clip types (big plays, funny, clutch, etc.)?
- **Timestamp Accuracy (5 pts)** - Are start/end times precise with appropriate buffer?

## Content Quality (25 points)

- **Title Effectiveness (12 pts)** - Are titles engaging, accurate, and optimized for clicks?
- **Context Clarity (8 pts)** - Is it clear what happened and why it's clip-worthy?
- **Energy Assessment (5 pts)** - Are excitement ratings accurate and helpful?

## Platform Optimization (20 points)

- **Platform Fit (10 pts)** - Are recommendations tailored to TikTok/YouTube/Twitch audiences?
- **Length Guidance (5 pts)** - Are clip lengths appropriate for each platform?
- **Editing Suggestions (5 pts)** - Are practical editing tips provided (trim points, captions)?

## Discoverability (15 points)

- **Thumbnail Selection (8 pts)** - Are thumbnail freeze-frames chosen strategically?
- **Hashtag Strategy (7 pts)** - Are relevant, trending hashtags suggested?

## Presentation (10 points)

- **Organization (5 pts)** - Are clips ranked and presented clearly?
- **Efficiency (5 pts)** - Can the streamer quickly execute on these recommendations?

## Scoring Guidelines

- **90-100**: Clips that will definitely perform well with spot-on titles and platform strategy
- **80-89**: Strong clip selection with good titles and optimization
- **70-79**: Decent moments but weak titles or platform mismatch
- **60-69**: Mediocre clip selection or unclear optimization strategy
- **Below 60**: Missing obvious moments or poor platform understanding
