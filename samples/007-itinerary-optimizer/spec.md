# Itinerary Optimizer — Specification

## Goal
Build a day-by-day travel itinerary that maximizes experiences while minimizing transit time, respecting budget and energy levels.

## Target Audience
Travelers planning trips

## Requirements
1. Day-by-day schedule from arrival to departure
2. Geographical clustering to minimize transit between activities
3. Mix of must-see attractions and local hidden gems
4. Restaurant recommendations near each day's activities
5. Estimated costs per day and running total
6. Buffer time for spontaneity (at least 2 hours per day)
7. Backup indoor options for weather-dependent activities
8. Transit instructions between locations

## Output Format
Trip overview with budget summary, then daily itinerary cards with times, locations, costs, and transit notes

## Quality Criteria
- Geography is logical (no zigzagging)
- Budget is tracked accurately
- Pace is sustainable
- Local gems included alongside tourist spots
