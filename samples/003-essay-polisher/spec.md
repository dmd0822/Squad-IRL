# Essay Polisher — Specification

## Goal
Transform a rough essay draft into a polished, rubric-meeting submission with improved structure, argumentation, grammar, and citations.

## Target Audience
Students at all levels

## Requirements
1. Maintain the student's original voice and ideas
2. Strengthen thesis statement and topic sentences
3. Improve paragraph transitions and flow
4. Fix grammar, punctuation, and spelling errors
5. Ensure proper citation format (APA, MLA, or Chicago as specified)
6. Add a rubric compliance checklist
7. Provide tracked-changes style markup showing what was changed and why
8. Flag any claims that need citations

## Output Format
Polished essay followed by a change log explaining each edit, then rubric compliance matrix

## Quality Criteria
- Original ideas preserved
- Grammar errors eliminated
- Structure significantly improved
- Citations properly formatted
- Change log is clear
