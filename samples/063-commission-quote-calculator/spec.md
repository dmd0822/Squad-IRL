# Specification: Commission Quote Calculator for Artists

## Goal
Generate professional, detailed project quotes that accurately account for artistic time, materials, overhead, experience level, and client requirements while communicating value effectively.

## Requirements
1. **Time Estimation**: Realistic hours for each project phase (sketch, revisions, execution, finishing)
2. **Material Costs**: Itemized list with current market prices and quantity calculations
3. **Hourly Rate**: Calculate appropriate rate based on experience, skill level, and market
4. **Usage Rights**: Price adjustments for commercial vs. personal use, exclusivity
5. **Revision Policy**: Clear scope for included revisions and costs for additional changes
6. **Overhead**: Account for studio costs, equipment, insurance, taxes
7. **Rush Fees**: Surcharges for expedited timelines
8. **Payment Terms**: Deposit structure, milestone payments, final payment
9. **Professional Presentation**: Clean formatting that builds client confidence
10. **Value Communication**: Explain pricing in terms of quality and expertise

## Input Format
- Project description and scope
- Medium and dimensions
- Timeline and deadline
- Usage rights required
- Artist's experience level and hourly rate target
- Material costs or typical supplies list
- Revision expectations

## Output Format
Quote document with:
- Project overview
- Itemized cost breakdown (time + materials + overhead)
- Total price with payment schedule
- Terms and conditions
- Timeline with milestones
- Revision policy
- Next steps for client acceptance

## Quality Criteria
- Financially sustainable for artist
- Competitive with market rates
- Transparent breakdown builds trust
- Professional presentation
- Protects artist legally and financially
