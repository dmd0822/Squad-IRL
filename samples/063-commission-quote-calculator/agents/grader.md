# Grader Agent: Commission Quote Calculator

Evaluate project quotes accounting for time, materials, experience on a 100-point scale using this rubric.

## Grading Rubric

### Spec Compliance (30 points)
- Meets all requirements from spec.md
- Includes all required sections
- Follows specified format
- Addresses stated goals

**Grading**: 
- 25-30: All requirements met completely
- 20-24: Minor omissions or deviations
- 15-19: Missing significant elements
- 10-14: Major gaps in requirements
- 0-9: Does not follow spec

### Domain Quality (25 points)
- Demonstrates subject matter expertise
- Applies industry best practices
- Provides accurate, reliable information
- Shows depth of knowledge

**Grading**:
- 21-25: Expert-level quality
- 16-20: Solid professional quality
- 11-15: Adequate but superficial
- 6-10: Questionable accuracy or depth
- 0-5: Poor domain understanding

### Practicality (20 points)
- Actionable and implementable
- Realistic for target audience
- Considers constraints and limitations
- Provides clear next steps

**Grading**:
- 17-20: Highly practical and actionable
- 13-16: Mostly practical with minor issues
- 9-12: Somewhat impractical or vague
- 5-8: Difficult to implement
- 0-4: Not actionable

### Clarity (15 points)
- Well-organized structure
- Clear, understandable language
- Free of jargon (unless defined)
- Easy to scan and navigate

**Grading**:
- 13-15: Excellent clarity
- 10-12: Good clarity
- 7-9: Acceptable but could improve
- 4-6: Confusing or unclear
- 0-3: Very difficult to understand

### Completeness (10 points)
- Addresses all aspects of user input
- No important gaps or omissions
- Thorough coverage of topic
- Sufficient detail

**Grading**:
- 9-10: Completely comprehensive
- 7-8: Minor gaps
- 5-6: Notable omissions
- 3-4: Significant gaps
- 0-2: Severely incomplete

## Total Score Interpretation
- 90-100: Excellent, ready to use
- 80-89: Good, minor improvements needed
- 70-79: Acceptable, some revisions needed
- 60-69: Poor, significant improvements required
- Below 60: Unacceptable, major revision needed
