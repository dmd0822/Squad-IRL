# Specification: Debt Payoff Optimizer

## Goal
Create personalized debt payoff plans using avalanche or snowball methods that minimize interest, maintain motivation, and achieve financial freedom on realistic timelines.

## Requirements
1. **Debt Inventory**: Complete list with balances, interest rates, minimum payments
2. **Method Comparison**: Show avalanche (highest interest first) vs. snowball (smallest balance first)
3. **Payment Strategy**: Optimize extra payment allocation for fastest payoff
4. **Interest Savings**: Calculate total interest paid under different scenarios
5. **Timeline**: Month-by-month payoff schedule with milestones
6. **Budget Integration**: Ensure plan fits within monthly budget constraints
7. **Motivation Tracking**: Celebrate wins and visualize progress
8. **Scenario Planning**: Show impact of extra payments or windfalls
9. **Emergency Fund**: Balance debt payoff with maintaining savings cushion
10. **Behavioral Economics**: Incorporate psychological factors (momentum, quick wins)

## Input Format
- List of debts (type, balance, interest rate, minimum payment)
- Monthly budget and available extra payment amount
- Payoff priorities (speed vs. psychological wins)
- Emergency fund status
- Expected windfalls or income changes

## Output Format
Debt payoff plan with:
- Side-by-side method comparison (avalanche vs. snowball)
- Recommended strategy with rationale
- Month-by-month payment schedule
- Milestone celebrations (debt-free dates)
- Total interest comparison
- Payoff timeline visualization
- What-if scenarios (extra $100/month, $1000 windfall)

## Quality Criteria
- Mathematically accurate projections
- Realistic for user's budget
- Balances optimization with psychology
- Clear action steps
- Motivating without being overwhelming
