# Expense Categorizer — Specification

## Goal
Analyze a month of bank transactions and produce a categorized spending report with insights, trends, and actionable savings recommendations.

## Target Audience
Everyone managing personal finances

## Requirements
1. Categorize transactions into: Housing, Food, Transport, Entertainment, Health, Shopping, Utilities, Subscriptions, Other
2. Flag recurring charges and subscriptions
3. Identify unusual or potentially fraudulent transactions
4. Calculate percentage of income spent per category
5. Compare to recommended budget percentages (50/30/20 rule)
6. Highlight top 5 areas where spending could be reduced
7. Show month-over-month trend if previous data available

## Output Format
Executive summary, category breakdown table, subscription audit, savings opportunities, and visual spending distribution

## Quality Criteria
- Every transaction categorized
- Math is correct
- Recommendations are specific and actionable
- Sensitive data redacted
