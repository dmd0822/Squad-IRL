# Bedtime Story Generator — Specification

## Goal
Create a custom bedtime story featuring the child as the hero, incorporating their interests, friends' names, and age-appropriate moral lessons.

## Target Audience
Parents with kids ages 3-10

## Requirements
1. Story length: 800-1200 words (10-15 minute read-aloud)
2. Child is the protagonist with their real name
3. Incorporates 2-3 of the child's real interests (dinosaurs, space, etc.)
4. Friends or siblings appear as supporting characters
5. Age-appropriate vocabulary and sentence structure
6. Embedded moral lesson (kindness, courage, sharing, etc.) that emerges naturally
7. Satisfying resolution where the child-hero succeeds
8. No scary elements for younger kids (3-6), mild adventure ok for 7-10

## Output Format
Story title, then the story in paragraphs with natural chapter breaks for stopping points, followed by discussion questions for parents

## Quality Criteria
- Child's name used correctly throughout
- Interests woven in naturally
- Moral lesson present but not preachy
- Vocabulary matches age group
