# Negotiation Script Preparer Specification

## Goal
Prepare negotiation scripts with opening statements, counterarguments, and fallback positions for salary, freelance rates, home prices, or other negotiations.

## Requirements

1. **Context Analysis** - Understand negotiation type, parties involved, leverage situation, and market data
2. **Opening Position** - Craft confident opening statement with justification
3. **Anchor Setting** - Suggest strategic first number (when to go first vs. let them offer)
4. **Counterargument Prep** - Anticipate 5-8 common objections with scripted responses
5. **Evidence Compilation** - Gather market data, comps, or benchmarks to support position
6. **Range Strategy** - Define ideal, target, and walk-away numbers
7. **Non-Monetary Value** - Identify alternatives (equity, PTO, title, flexible hours, closing costs)
8. **Silence Strategy** - Coach when to wait, when to ask questions, when to pivot
9. **Relationship Preservation** - Maintain collaborative tone throughout scripts
10. **Walk-Away Plan** - Clear criteria and graceful exit script if negotiation fails

## Output Format

```markdown
# Negotiation Prep: [Type] with [Party]

## Your Situation
[Summary: leverage, timeline, alternatives, must-haves]

## Market Data
[Relevant comps, benchmarks, or research]

## Your Numbers
- **Ideal**: $X
- **Target**: $Y (realistic goal)
- **Walk-Away**: $Z (absolute minimum)

## Opening Script
[Confident opening statement]

## Common Objections & Your Responses
1. "That's above our budget" → [Response]
2. "We can't do that for someone at your level" → [Response]
...

## Non-Monetary Alternatives
[What else you can negotiate if numbers are fixed]

## Silence & Pivot Points
[When to pause, when to ask questions, when to pivot to alternatives]

## Walk-Away Script
[Graceful way to decline if your minimums aren't met]
```

## Quality Criteria

- **Confidence**: Scripts project competence without arrogance
- **Data-Backed**: All positions supported by market research or evidence
- **Flexibility**: Multiple paths forward with alternatives prepared
- **Realism**: Numbers and asks are ambitious but defensible
- **Relationship-Preserving**: Tone remains collaborative, not adversarial
