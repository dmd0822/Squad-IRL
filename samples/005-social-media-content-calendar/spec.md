# Social Media Content Calendar — Specification

## Goal
Generate 30 days of on-brand social media content with captions, hashtags, posting times, and content type mix across platforms.

## Target Audience
Small business owners

## Requirements
1. 30 days of daily posts for Instagram, Twitter/X, and LinkedIn
2. Mix of content types: educational (40%), entertaining (30%), promotional (20%), community (10%)
3. Platform-specific formatting (character limits, hashtag counts, image suggestions)
4. Consistent brand voice across all posts
5. Relevant trending hashtags mixed with branded hashtags
6. Optimal posting times based on platform best practices
7. Weekly themes for content cohesion
8. At least 3 engagement-driving posts (polls, questions, CTAs) per week

## Output Format
Calendar grid view by week, then individual post cards with platform, caption, hashtags, posting time, and content type

## Quality Criteria
- 90 posts total (30 days × 3 platforms)
- Content mix matches percentages
- No repeated captions
- Hashtags are relevant and current
