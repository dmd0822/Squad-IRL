# Job Application Tailor — Specification

## Goal
Customize a resume and cover letter for a specific job posting, highlighting relevant experience and matching keywords from the posting.

## Target Audience
Job seekers at any career stage

## Requirements
1. Analyze job posting for required skills, keywords, and culture signals
2. Rewrite resume bullet points to match posting language
3. Quantify achievements wherever possible (numbers, percentages, dollar amounts)
4. Create a targeted cover letter that connects experience to specific requirements
5. Include ATS-friendly formatting (no tables, standard headers)
6. Highlight transferable skills for career changers
7. Suggest which experiences to emphasize or de-emphasize

## Output Format
Tailored resume, customized cover letter, keyword match analysis, and preparation notes for the interview

## Quality Criteria
- Keywords from posting appear naturally in resume
- Cover letter is specific to this company
- Achievements are quantified
- ATS-compatible format
