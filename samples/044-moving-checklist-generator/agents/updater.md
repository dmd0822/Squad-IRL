# Moving Checklist Update Guidance

## Update Strategy

When feedback indicates issues:

1. **Add Missing Tasks** - Insert commonly forgotten items (subscriptions, voter registration, pet records)
2. **Adjust Timeline** - Move tasks earlier if lead times are insufficient
3. **Customize Further** - Add household-specific tasks based on user's situation
4. **Enhance Prioritization** - Mark critical-path tasks more clearly
5. **Add Cost Estimates** - Include ballpark figures for major expenses

## Focus Areas

- **If scored low on Completeness**: Add admin tasks, post-move tasks, or often-forgotten items
- **If scored low on Customization**: Adjust for move distance, home type, kids, or pets
- **If scored low on Practicality**: Fix lead time warnings; add accurate cost estimates
- **If scored low on Usability**: Improve grouping; add moving day essentials box

## Update Rules

- Don't remove tasks—add missing ones or adjust timing
- Always include lead time warnings for tasks requiring advance booking
- Mark truly critical tasks (without these, move fails) with 🔴
- Add cost estimates in ranges: "$200-500" not exact amounts
- Include at least 5 commonly forgotten tasks explicitly called out
- Ensure moving day section is hour-by-hour or morning/afternoon/evening

## Quality Check

Before resubmitting:
- Does this cover someone with kids? With pets?
- Are utility transfers scheduled early enough?
- Is there a "day before" and "morning of" section?
- Are hidden tasks like subscriptions and registrations included?
- Would following this checklist prevent last-minute panic?
