# Moving Checklist Grading Rubric

## Completeness (30 points)

- **Task Coverage (15 pts)** - Are all major categories covered (admin, utilities, packing, cleaning, etc.)?
- **Hidden Tasks (10 pts)** - Are often-forgotten tasks included (mail forwarding, subscriptions, voter registration)?
- **Post-Move Tasks (5 pts)** - Does checklist extend through first 2 weeks in new home?

## Customization (25 points)

- **Move Type Adaptation (10 pts)** - Is checklist appropriate for local vs. long-distance move?
- **Household Complexity (10 pts)** - Are tasks adjusted for home size, kids, pets, etc.?
- **Timeline Accuracy (5 pts)** - Are tasks scheduled with appropriate lead times?

## Practicality (20 points)

- **Realistic Timing (8 pts)** - Are lead times accurate (e.g., 6+ weeks for summer movers)?
- **Cost Transparency (7 pts)** - Are major expenses estimated accurately?
- **Prioritization (5 pts)** - Are critical tasks clearly distinguished from nice-to-haves?

## Usability (15 points)

- **Organization (8 pts)** - Are tasks logically grouped and sequenced?
- **Moving Day Clarity (7 pts)** - Is moving day itself clearly structured with essentials list?

## Stress Reduction (10 points)

- **Troubleshooting (5 pts)** - Are common problems and solutions included?
- **Essentials Planning (5 pts)** - Is there guidance on what to keep accessible?

## Scoring Guidelines

- **90-100**: Professional-grade checklist that prevents chaos and forgotten tasks
- **80-89**: Solid, comprehensive checklist covering most scenarios
- **70-79**: Useful but missing some hidden tasks or customization
- **60-69**: Generic checklist with timing issues or missing categories
- **Below 60**: Incomplete or poorly sequenced—would miss critical deadlines
