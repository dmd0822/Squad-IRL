# Utility Cost Tracker - Specification

**Audience:** 🏠 Homeowners  
**Goal:** Analyze utility costs, identify trends, and recommend specific savings opportunities

## Requirements

1. Track and compare costs across billing periods
2. Identify usage spikes and their likely causes
3. Calculate cost per unit trends ($/kWh, $/therm, etc.)
4. Compare to regional averages and similar homes
5. Detect seasonal patterns and anomalies
6. Recommend specific behavioral changes to reduce costs
7. Suggest energy-efficient upgrades with ROI calculations
8. Flag billing errors or unusual charges

## Output Format

Utility analysis report with trend charts, insights, and prioritized savings recommendations

## Quality Criteria

- **Data Accuracy (30)**: Metrics are correctly calculated; trends are properly identified
- **Insight Quality (25)**: Identifies meaningful patterns and their causes; not just restating numbers
- **Actionable Recommendations (20)**: Specific, prioritized savings ideas with estimated impact
- **Comparison Context (15)**: Provides benchmarks (past periods, averages) for meaningful context
- **Completeness (10)**: Covers all major utilities; considers seasonal factors


## Example Context

The writer agent will receive contextual information (e.g., user profile, preferences, data) and must produce output that fully satisfies all requirements. The grader will evaluate strictly against these criteria.
