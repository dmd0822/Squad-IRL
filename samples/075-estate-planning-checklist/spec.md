# Specification: Estate Planning Checklist Generator

## Goal
Create comprehensive, personalized estate planning task lists that guide seniors through organizing assets, documents, and wishes while demystifying the process and prioritizing essential steps.

## Requirements
1. **Complete Scope**: Cover wills, trusts, beneficiaries, power of attorney, healthcare directives
2. **Prioritization**: Order tasks by importance and urgency
3. **Document Inventory**: Checklist of essential papers to locate and organize
4. **Asset Mapping**: Guide for listing accounts, property, valuables
5. **Decision Prompts**: Questions to consider for key choices (executors, guardians, distributions)
6. **Professional Guidance**: Flag tasks requiring attorney, accountant, or financial advisor
7. **Digital Assets**: Include online accounts, passwords, digital property
8. **Family Communication**: Tasks around discussing plans with family
9. **Maintenance Reminders**: When to review and update documents
10. **State-Specific**: Note when state laws affect requirements

## Input Format
- Age and location (state)
- Family situation (marital status, children, dependents)
- Asset complexity (simple vs. complex estate)
- Existing documents (if any)
- Specific concerns (special needs, business, blended family)
- Timeline preferences

## Output Format
Estate planning checklist with:
- Prioritized task list with checkboxes
- Document inventory to gather
- Asset inventory worksheet
- Key decisions to make (with guiding questions)
- Professional help needed (when to hire lawyer/advisor)
- Family communication plan
- Timeline and milestones
- State-specific requirements
- Digital asset checklist
- Review and update schedule

## Quality Criteria
- Comprehensive but not overwhelming
- Clear guidance on DIY vs. professional help
- Addresses individual's specific situation
- Reduces fear through organization
- Legally sound recommendations
