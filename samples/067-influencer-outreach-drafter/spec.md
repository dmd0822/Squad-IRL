# Specification: Influencer Outreach Drafter

## Goal
Craft personalized, compelling partnership pitch emails to influencers that demonstrate genuine interest, articulate mutual value, and increase likelihood of positive response.

## Requirements
1. **Personalization**: Reference specific content, values, or posts from influencer
2. **Brand Alignment**: Explain why partnership makes sense for their audience
3. **Value Proposition**: Clear benefits for influencer (compensation, product, exposure, creative freedom)
4. **Campaign Details**: Scope, timeline, deliverables, and expectations
5. **Tone Matching**: Mirror influencer's communication style (casual, professional, etc.)
6. **Authenticity**: Avoid generic, copy-paste feel
7. **Easy Response**: Clear CTA and low-friction next steps
8. **Concise**: Respect their time with scannable format
9. **Professionalism**: Error-free writing that reflects brand quality
10. **Follow-up Strategy**: Plan for non-response or negotiation

## Input Format
- Influencer name, handle, platform, follower count
- Links to recent content or portfolio
- Brand/product details
- Campaign goals and parameters
- Budget and compensation structure
- Timeline and deadlines
- Brand voice guidelines

## Output Format
Email pitch with:
- Compelling subject line
- Personalized greeting and hook
- Brief brand introduction
- Why this influencer specifically
- Campaign overview and deliverables
- Compensation and benefits
- Creative freedom statement
- Clear call-to-action
- Contact information
- Optional: Follow-up template

## Quality Criteria
- Feels personal, not templated
- Demonstrates knowledge of influencer's work
- Respects creative autonomy
- Fair compensation clearly stated
- Professional yet approachable tone
