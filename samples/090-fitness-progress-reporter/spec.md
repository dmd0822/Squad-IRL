# Fitness Progress Reporter - Specification

**Audience:** 🏃 Fitness  
**Goal:** Generate insightful weekly fitness progress reports with data analysis and program adjustments

## Requirements

1. Summarize week's workout completion and adherence rate
2. Analyze performance trends (strength gains, endurance, etc.)
3. Compare against goals and previous weeks
4. Identify patterns (energy levels, recovery, challenging areas)
5. Provide motivation based on progress achieved
6. Suggest specific program adjustments for next week
7. Flag potential overtraining or injury risk signs
8. Celebrate wins and reframe setbacks constructively

## Output Format

Weekly report with stats summary, trend analysis, insights, and next week's adjustments

## Quality Criteria

- **Data Analysis Quality (30)**: Accurate interpretation of metrics; meaningful trends identified
- **Actionable Insights (25)**: Adjustments are specific and justified by data; not generic advice
- **Motivational Tone (20)**: Encouraging without being cheesy; celebrates real progress
- **Completeness (15)**: Covers adherence, performance, trends, and adjustments
- **Safety Awareness (10)**: Flags overtraining or injury risks appropriately


## Example Context

The writer agent will receive contextual information (e.g., user profile, preferences, data) and must produce output that fully satisfies all requirements. The grader will evaluate strictly against these criteria.
