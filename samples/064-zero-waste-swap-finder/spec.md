# Specification: Zero-Waste Swap Finder

## Goal
Recommend eco-friendly product alternatives for everyday items that reduce waste, environmental impact, and often save money, with practical implementation guidance.

## Requirements
1. **Product Matching**: Suggest swaps for specific conventional products user currently buys
2. **Impact Metrics**: Quantify waste reduction (plastic saved, water conserved, CO2 avoided)
3. **Cost Comparison**: Initial investment vs. long-term savings analysis
4. **Ease Ranking**: Rate difficulty of switching (easy/moderate/challenging)
5. **Quality Assessment**: Compare performance to conventional products
6. **Local Availability**: Consider where to purchase (online, local stores, bulk shops)
7. **Lifestyle Fit**: Account for living situation (apartment vs. house, family size)
8. **DIY Options**: Include homemade alternatives where practical
9. **Avoid Greenwashing**: Verify authentic environmental benefits
10. **Incremental Approach**: Prioritize high-impact, easy swaps first

## Input Format
- List of products/categories to replace
- Budget constraints
- Living situation and lifestyle factors
- Environmental priorities (plastic, water, carbon, etc.)
- Access to bulk stores, farmers markets

## Output Format
Swap guide with:
- Product comparison table (conventional vs. eco alternative)
- Environmental impact calculations per swap
- Cost analysis (upfront + annual savings)
- Ease-of-adoption rating
- Where to buy and estimated prices
- Implementation tips
- Prioritized action plan (start with these 3 swaps)

## Quality Criteria
- Measurable environmental benefits
- Realistic for average consumer
- Cost-effective or cost-neutral long-term
- Quality alternatives that work well
- Avoiding perfectionism or eco-anxiety
