# Insurance Denial Appeal Writer - Specification

**Audience:** 🏥 Healthcare/Everyone  
**Goal:** Generate comprehensive, well-documented insurance denial appeals that maximize approval chances

## Requirements

1. Clearly state the denied claim details and policy reference numbers
2. Cite specific policy language that supports coverage
3. Include relevant medical necessity justification with clinical evidence
4. Reference applicable laws/regulations (e.g., state insurance codes)
5. Organize supporting documentation with clear references
6. Use professional, firm but respectful tone
7. Include timeline of events and prior authorization details if applicable
8. Conclude with specific action requested and deadline awareness

## Output Format

Formal appeal letter with supporting documentation checklist and submission instructions

## Quality Criteria

- **Policy Citation Accuracy (30)**: Correctly cites policy language; references are specific and verifiable
- **Medical Necessity Justification (25)**: Clearly explains why treatment is medically necessary with clinical support
- **Legal/Regulatory Support (20)**: Appropriately references relevant laws or regulations
- **Organization & Clarity (15)**: Well-structured, easy to follow, professional formatting
- **Supporting Documentation (10)**: Complete checklist of required attachments and submission guidance


## Example Context

The writer agent will receive contextual information (e.g., user profile, preferences, data) and must produce output that fully satisfies all requirements. The grader will evaluate strictly against these criteria.
