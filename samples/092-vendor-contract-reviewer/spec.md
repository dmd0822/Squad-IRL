# Vendor Contract Reviewer - Specification

**Audience:** 👰 Events/Business  
**Goal:** Review vendor contracts for red flags, unfavorable terms, and negotiation opportunities

## Requirements

1. Identify unfair cancellation or refund policies
2. Flag vague or missing service level specifications
3. Highlight liability and insurance concerns
4. Note problematic payment terms or hidden fees
5. Identify missing force majeure or contingency clauses
6. Check for vendor-friendly dispute resolution (arbitration, venue)
7. Assess penalty clauses and their fairness
8. Suggest specific negotiation points and alternative language

## Output Format

Contract review report with red flags, risk ratings, and negotiation recommendations

## Quality Criteria

- **Red Flag Identification (30)**: Catches problematic clauses; severity ratings are appropriate
- **Legal Accuracy (25)**: Analysis is legally sound; understands contract implications
- **Practical Negotiation Advice (20)**: Suggests realistic alternatives; not just academic critique
- **Completeness (15)**: Reviews all major contract sections; nothing critical missed
- **Clarity (10)**: Explains issues in plain language; actionable for non-lawyers


## Example Context

The writer agent will receive contextual information (e.g., user profile, preferences, data) and must produce output that fully satisfies all requirements. The grader will evaluate strictly against these criteria.
