# Meal Prep Squad — Specification

## Goal
Generate a complete weekly meal plan with recipes, grocery list, and prep schedule that respects nutrition goals, budget constraints, and picky eaters.

## Target Audience
Parents and families

## Requirements
1. 7 dinners + 5 lunches + 7 breakfasts for a family of 4
2. Calorie targets per family member (adults vs kids)
3. Maximum weekly grocery budget of $150
4. At least 2 vegetarian meals per week
5. No repeated main proteins within the same week
6. Prep time under 45 minutes for weeknight dinners
7. Consolidated grocery list organized by store section
8. Sunday prep schedule for batch cooking

## Output Format
Weekly calendar view with each meal, followed by recipe cards, then grocery list, then prep timeline

## Quality Criteria
- Nutritionally balanced across the week
- Realistic prep times
- Budget adds up correctly
- Kid-friendly options included
- Grocery list has no duplicates
