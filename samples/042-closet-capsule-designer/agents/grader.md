# Capsule Wardrobe Grading Rubric

## Versatility & Mix-Match (30 points)

- **Outfit Combinations (15 pts)** - Does the capsule generate 30+ distinct outfits from 15-30 pieces?
- **Piece Efficiency (10 pts)** - Does each item pair with at least 5 others?
- **Formula Variety (5 pts)** - Are outfit formulas provided for different occasions/moods?

## Color & Cohesion (25 points)

- **Palette Harmony (12 pts)** - Do all 4-6 colors work together? Are undertones consistent?
- **Mix-Match Logic (8 pts)** - Can any top genuinely be worn with any bottom?
- **User Suitability (5 pts)** - Do colors suit the user's coloring and preferences?

## Practical Realism (20 points)

- **Lifestyle Fit (10 pts)** - Does the capsule match the user's actual daily needs?
- **Body Type Consideration (5 pts)** - Are proportions and silhouettes flattering?
- **Maintenance Reality (5 pts)** - Are care requirements reasonable for the user?

## Actionability (15 points)

- **Clear Edits (8 pts)** - Is it clear what to remove, store, or keep?
- **Gap Identification (7 pts)** - Are missing pieces identified with specific recommendations?

## Organization & Clarity (10 points)

- **Visual Clarity (5 pts)** - Are outfit combinations easy to understand and visualize?
- **Structure (5 pts)** - Is the plan organized logically (by category, occasion, etc.)?

## Scoring Guidelines

- **90-100**: A stylist-grade capsule that truly simplifies wardrobe decisions
- **80-89**: Solid capsule with good versatility and clear action plan
- **70-79**: Useful but some pieces don't mix well or lifestyle mismatch
- **60-69**: Too many pieces, weak color cohesion, or impractical suggestions
- **Below 60**: Not a true capsule—lacks cohesion or versatility
