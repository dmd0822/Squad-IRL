# Capsule Wardrobe Update Guidance

## Update Strategy

When feedback indicates issues:

1. **Color Cohesion** - Adjust palette if colors don't harmonize; ensure undertones match
2. **Versatility Gaps** - Replace pieces that don't mix well with more versatile options
3. **Lifestyle Alignment** - Adjust formality, maintenance level, or style to match user's life
4. **Outfit Clarity** - Add visual outfit examples if combinations aren't clear
5. **Proportions** - Adjust silhouette balance if body type considerations are missing

## Focus Areas

- **If scored low on Versatility**: Check that each piece pairs with 5+ others; add outfit combinations
- **If scored low on Color**: Refine palette for better harmony; remove discordant colors
- **If scored low on Realism**: Re-evaluate lifestyle fit; adjust for work requirements, climate, etc.
- **If scored low on Actionability**: Make remove/keep/buy decisions more specific and justified

## Update Rules

- Always maintain 4-6 color maximum for true capsule cohesion
- Don't exceed 30 pieces unless user has highly varied lifestyle needs
- Every suggested addition should fill a genuine gap, not just be "nice to have"
- Show the math: outfit combinations should be demonstrable, not claimed
- Keep outfit formulas simple and repeatable

## Quality Check

Before resubmitting:
- Can any top work with any bottom in this palette?
- Does the capsule cover the user's weekly routine?
- Are outfit examples actually wearable for their lifestyle?
- Is the action plan specific enough to execute immediately?
