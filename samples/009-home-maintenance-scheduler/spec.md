# Home Maintenance Scheduler — Specification

## Goal
Generate a yearly home maintenance calendar with seasonal tasks, estimated costs, DIY vs professional recommendations, and priority levels.

## Target Audience
Homeowners

## Requirements
1. 12-month calendar with tasks assigned to specific months
2. Tasks categorized: HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical, Exterior, Interior, Appliances, Safety
3. Each task has: description, estimated time, estimated cost, DIY difficulty (1-5)
4. Professional vs DIY recommendation for each task
5. Seasonal preparation checklists (winterize, spring prep, etc.)
6. Annual budget estimate
7. Emergency contact list template for contractors

## Output Format
Month-by-month task calendar, then detailed task cards by category, then annual budget summary

## Quality Criteria
- Seasonal timing is correct
- Cost estimates are reasonable
- DIY difficulty ratings accurate
- Nothing critical is missed
