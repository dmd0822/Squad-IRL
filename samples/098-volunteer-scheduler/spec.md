# Volunteer Scheduler - Specification

**Audience:** 🏘️ Community  
**Goal:** Create optimized volunteer schedules matching skills and availability to organizational needs

## Requirements

1. Match volunteer skills to appropriate roles/tasks
2. Respect availability constraints and preferences
3. Ensure adequate coverage for all time slots/roles
4. Balance workload fairly across volunteers
5. Account for training requirements before assignment
6. Provide clear role descriptions and expectations
7. Include contact info and shift logistics
8. Generate automated reminders and confirmations

## Output Format

Volunteer schedule with skill-matched assignments, coverage report, and communication templates

## Quality Criteria

- **Skill Matching (30)**: Volunteers are assigned to roles matching their abilities and interests
- **Coverage & Balance (25)**: All slots filled; workload is fair; no burnout risk
- **Practical Logistics (20)**: Clear role descriptions, times, locations, and expectations
- **Flexibility (15)**: Accounts for constraints; includes backup plans
- **Communication (10)**: Includes confirmation and reminder templates


## Example Context

The writer agent will receive contextual information (e.g., user profile, preferences, data) and must produce output that fully satisfies all requirements. The grader will evaluate strictly against these criteria.
