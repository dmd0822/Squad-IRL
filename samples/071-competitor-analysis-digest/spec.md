# Specification: Competitor Analysis Digest

## Goal
Monitor competitors systematically and deliver weekly insights highlighting changes, opportunities, and strategic implications for small business decision-making.

## Requirements
1. **Structured Monitoring**: Track pricing, products, marketing, reviews, hiring, news
2. **Change Detection**: Identify what's new or different from last week
3. **Strategic Implications**: Explain what changes mean for your business
4. **Opportunity Spotting**: Find gaps or weaknesses to exploit
5. **Threat Assessment**: Flag competitive moves requiring response
6. **Trend Synthesis**: Connect dots across multiple competitors
7. **Actionable Insights**: Specific recommendations, not just observations
8. **Prioritization**: Rank findings by importance and urgency
9. **Data Sources**: List where information came from (website, social, reviews, etc.)
10. **Concise Format**: Digestible in 10-15 minutes for busy owners

## Input Format
- List of competitors to monitor
- Your business category and unique positioning
- Data sources to check (websites, social media, review sites)
- Previous week's digest for comparison
- Specific areas of focus (pricing, product launches, marketing)

## Output Format
Weekly digest with:
- Executive summary (top 3 insights)
- Competitor-by-competitor breakdown of changes
- Strategic implications and opportunities
- Recommended actions (immediate and longer-term)
- Trend analysis across competitors
- Threats to address
- Sources and links
- Next week's focus areas

## Quality Criteria
- Focuses on actionable intelligence
- Distinguishes signal from noise
- Clear strategic thinking
- Realistic recommendations for small business
- Saves owner time vs. manual monitoring
