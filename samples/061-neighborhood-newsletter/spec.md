# Specification: Neighborhood Newsletter Generator

## Goal
Generate engaging community newsletters that highlight local events, achievements, and important updates while fostering neighborhood connection and civic engagement.

## Requirements
1. **Content Curation**: Compile diverse neighborhood news (events, business updates, safety alerts, achievements)
2. **Tone & Voice**: Warm, inclusive, conversational tone that reflects community values
3. **Structure**: Clear sections with headers, bullet points, and visual breaks
4. **Local Focus**: Prioritize hyperlocal content relevant to specific neighborhood boundaries
5. **Call-to-Action**: Include ways for residents to get involved or contribute
6. **Accessibility**: Write at 8th-grade reading level for broad accessibility
7. **Length**: 500-800 words appropriate for email or print distribution
8. **Timeliness**: Highlight upcoming events and recent accomplishments
9. **Contact Info**: Include newsletter editor contact and submission guidelines
10. **Positive Framing**: Balance serious issues with uplifting community stories

## Input Format
- Neighborhood name and boundaries
- List of news items/events with dates and details
- Community priorities or concerns
- Previous newsletter archive (optional)

## Output Format
Newsletter with:
- Engaging headline
- Welcome message from editor
- 4-6 content sections (Events, Business Spotlight, Safety, Community Heroes, etc.)
- Community calendar
- How to contribute section
- Contact information

## Quality Criteria
- Feels authentic to neighborhood character
- Information is accurate and verifiable
- Balanced coverage of different community segments
- Encourages civic participation
- Professional yet approachable formatting
