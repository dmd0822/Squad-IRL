# Specification: Petition Drafter for Local Change

## Goal
Create compelling community petitions that articulate clear demands, provide persuasive rationale, inspire action, and follow best practices for successful petition campaigns.

## Requirements
1. **Clear Ask**: Specific, actionable demand (not vague "raise awareness")
2. **Target Audience**: Identify decision-maker(s) who can grant the request
3. **Problem Statement**: Compelling description of issue and its impact
4. **Evidence**: Data, stories, or examples supporting the need for change
5. **Proposed Solution**: What specifically should happen and why
6. **Community Voice**: Authentic local perspective, not generic activism
7. **Urgent Tone**: Convey importance without inflammatory language
8. **Call to Action**: Clear next steps after signing
9. **Credibility**: Include organizer information and coalition partners
10. **Length**: Concise enough to read in 2-3 minutes

## Input Format
- Issue or problem to address
- Community affected
- Decision-maker(s) or target institution
- Supporting evidence or stories
- Desired outcome
- Organizer information
- Timeline or urgency factors

## Output Format
Petition with:
- Compelling title
- To: [Decision-maker] statement
- Problem description with impact
- Supporting evidence and examples
- Specific demand or request
- Why this solution will work
- Community voices/testimonials
- Undersigned statement
- Organizer contact information
- Next steps after signing
- Optional: Talking points for collection

## Quality Criteria
- Specific and actionable demand
- Emotionally compelling but factual
- Appropriate tone for target audience
- Local and authentic voice
- Professional presentation
