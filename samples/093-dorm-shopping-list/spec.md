# Dorm Shopping List Generator - Specification

**Audience:** 🎓 Students  
**Goal:** Create comprehensive, budget-optimized college dorm shopping lists tailored to specific campus rules

## Requirements

1. Categorize items (bedding, storage, electronics, supplies, etc.)
2. Mark essential vs. nice-to-have items
3. Account for what college provides vs. what student brings
4. Include dorm-specific restrictions (mini fridges, candles, etc.)
5. Optimize for budget with high/low price options
6. Suggest space-saving solutions for small rooms
7. Include roommate coordination items (share vs. duplicate)
8. Provide shopping timeline (what to buy when)

## Output Format

Categorized shopping list with priorities, budget tiers, and roommate coordination notes

## Quality Criteria

- **Completeness (30)**: Nothing essential is missing; categories are comprehensive
- **Budget Optimization (25)**: Clear priority tiers; provides cost-saving strategies
- **Practical Wisdom (20)**: Accounts for dorm restrictions, space limits, and college-specific rules
- **Roommate Coordination (15)**: Clearly marks sharable items; avoids duplication waste
- **Organization (10)**: Well-categorized; easy to shop from; includes timeline


## Example Context

The writer agent will receive contextual information (e.g., user profile, preferences, data) and must produce output that fully satisfies all requirements. The grader will evaluate strictly against these criteria.
