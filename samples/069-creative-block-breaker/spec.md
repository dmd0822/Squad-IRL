# Specification: Creative Block Breaker

## Goal
Generate personalized prompts, exercises, and strategies to help artists and writers overcome creative block, rebuild confidence, and reconnect with their creative practice.

## Requirements
1. **Block Diagnosis**: Identify type of block (fear, perfectionism, burnout, skill plateau)
2. **Personalized Exercises**: Tailor prompts to medium, skill level, and block cause
3. **Low-Stakes Entry**: Remove pressure with playful, exploratory activities
4. **Constraint-Based Creativity**: Use limitations to spark ideas (time, materials, theme)
5. **Skill-Building**: Address underlying skill gaps causing frustration
6. **Mindset Shifts**: Reframe perfectionism, comparison, or self-criticism
7. **Variety**: Offer 5-10 different exercises to match mood and energy
8. **Actionable**: Clear instructions that can be started immediately
9. **Progressive Challenge**: Build from warm-up exercises to deeper work
10. **Celebration**: Encourage completion and self-compassion

## Input Format
- Creative medium (writing, visual art, music, etc.)
- Current skill level
- Description of creative block symptoms
- Previous successful practices
- Available time and materials
- Specific projects feeling stuck on (optional)

## Output Format
Creative unblocking toolkit with:
- Block diagnosis and reassurance
- 5-10 exercises ranging from 5-minute warm-ups to deeper explorations
- Clear instructions for each exercise
- Why each exercise works (the psychology)
- Recommended order or choose-your-own-adventure
- Mindset tips and affirmations
- Next steps after completing exercises
- Resources for ongoing practice

## Quality Criteria
- Addresses specific type of creative block
- Exercises feel doable, not overwhelming
- Reduces pressure and perfectionism
- Backed by creative psychology principles
- Encouraging and compassionate tone
