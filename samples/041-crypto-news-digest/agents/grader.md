# Crypto News Digest Grading Rubric

## Signal Quality (30 points)

- **High Signal Accuracy (15 pts)** - Are truly important items correctly identified? Are market-moving events prioritized?
- **Noise Filtering (10 pts)** - Is hype/FUD correctly relegated to low priority? Are scams flagged?
- **Source Credibility (5 pts)** - Are sources properly weighted by reliability?

## Domain Expertise (25 points)

- **Technical Accuracy (10 pts)** - Are blockchain/crypto concepts explained correctly?
- **On-Chain Verification (8 pts)** - Are claims cross-referenced with actual blockchain data?
- **Market Context (7 pts)** - Is news placed in proper market context (bull/bear, macro factors)?

## Critical Analysis (20 points)

- **Scam Detection (8 pts)** - Are potential scams/manipulation identified and explained?
- **Claim Verification (7 pts)** - Are extraordinary claims properly fact-checked or marked unconfirmed?
- **Bias Recognition (5 pts)** - Are conflicts of interest or promotional content identified?

## Actionability (15 points)

- **Clear Implications (8 pts)** - Does each high-signal item explain "what this means"?
- **Trend Analysis (7 pts)** - Are emerging trends identified with supporting evidence?

## Clarity & Structure (10 points)

- **Organization (5 pts)** - Is content logically structured by signal strength?
- **Readability (5 pts)** - Are technical concepts accessible to informed readers?

## Scoring Guidelines

- **90-100**: Institutional-grade analysis that traders/investors can act on
- **80-89**: Solid digest that filters noise effectively
- **70-79**: Useful but missing key stories or including too much noise
- **60-69**: Some signal but too much hype or insufficient verification
- **Below 60**: Unreliable - fails to distinguish signal from noise
