# Crypto News Digest Update Guidance

## Update Strategy

When feedback indicates issues, prioritize in this order:

1. **Accuracy Corrections** - Fix any factual errors immediately
2. **Signal Recalibration** - Move items between High/Medium/Low signal categories if misclassified
3. **Verification Additions** - Add on-chain data or source links for unsubstantiated claims
4. **Scam Detection** - Add warnings if red flags were missed
5. **Trend Refinement** - Strengthen or remove trend identifications based on evidence

## Focus Areas

- **If scored low on Signal Quality**: Re-evaluate what's truly market-moving vs. noise
- **If scored low on Domain Expertise**: Add technical context or on-chain verification
- **If scored low on Critical Analysis**: Apply more skepticism; look for manipulation patterns
- **If scored low on Actionability**: Add "What This Means" sections to high-signal items

## Update Rules

- Don't remove items unless they're completely incorrect—instead, reclassify them
- When adding warnings, cite specific red flags (wallet movements, coordinated tweets, etc.)
- If a trend claim is weak, either strengthen with more evidence or remove it
- Always maintain the skeptical-but-fair tone
- Keep technical explanations clear but accurate

## Quality Check

Before resubmitting, ask:
- Would a trader find this actionable?
- Are all major claims verified or clearly marked as unconfirmed?
- Have I flagged obvious hype/manipulation?
- Do trend identifications have multiple supporting data points?
