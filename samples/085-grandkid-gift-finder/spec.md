# Grandkid Gift Finder - Specification

**Audience:** 👵 Seniors  
**Goal:** Recommend thoughtful, age-appropriate gifts for grandchildren based on current trends and interests

## Requirements

1. Filter by child's age and developmental stage
2. Incorporate current trends kids actually care about
3. Consider educational value and screen-time balance
4. Suggest both popular items and unique alternatives
5. Include safety considerations and quality brands
6. Provide price ranges and where to purchase
7. Offer gift ideas that encourage grandparent-grandchild interaction
8. Account for different interest profiles (sporty, creative, tech-savvy, etc.)

## Output Format

Curated gift list with age-appropriateness ratings, descriptions, and purchase links

## Quality Criteria

- **Age Appropriateness (30)**: All suggestions match developmental stage; safety considered
- **Trend Awareness (25)**: Reflects what kids actually want now; not outdated
- **Variety & Thoughtfulness (20)**: Mix of popular/unique, toys/experiences, solo/interactive
- **Practical Guidance (15)**: Clear descriptions, prices, where to buy; grandparent-friendly
- **Bonding Potential (10)**: Includes options that encourage quality time together


## Example Context

The writer agent will receive contextual information (e.g., user profile, preferences, data) and must produce output that fully satisfies all requirements. The grader will evaluate strictly against these criteria.
